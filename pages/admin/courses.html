Course Information

Course Duration

Most courses are semester-based (15 weeks) with a combination of lectures, tutorials, and practical sessions.

Class Sizes

We maintain optimal student-to-faculty ratios to ensure personalized attention and effective learning.

Learning Resources

Access to modern computer labs, online materials, and industry-standard software for hands-on learning.

Assessment

Continuous assessment through assignments, projects, tests, and examinations to ensure comprehensive evaluation.

Prerequisites & Requirements

General Requirements

  • Minimum of 5 O'Level credits including Mathematics and English Language
  • JAMB UTME score meeting university cutoff requirements
  • Post-UTME screening examination
  • Basic computer literacy (recommended)

Course-Specific Prerequisites

200 Level Courses

Successful completion of all 100 level courses with minimum CGPA of 2.0

300 Level Courses

Completion of prerequisite 200 level courses in relevant areas

400 Level Courses

Advanced courses requiring completion of foundational courses and project proposal

Postgraduate Courses

Bachelor's degree in related field with minimum second class lower division
