Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

We require a minimum of 5 O'Level credits including Mathematics and English Language, JAMB UTME score meeting university requirements, and successful completion of our Post-UTME screening.

How can I access course materials?

Students can access course materials through the student portal after logging in with their registration number and passcode provided upon admission.

What career opportunities are available?

Our graduates work in various roles including Systems Analyst, Database Administrator, IT Project Manager, Business Analyst, and many start their own tech companies.

Do you offer online courses?

Yes, we offer some courses in hybrid format with both online and in-person components, especially for working professionals in our postgraduate programs.

How can I schedule a campus visit?

You can schedule a campus visit by contacting our admissions office at least 48 hours in advance. We offer guided tours on weekdays.

What research opportunities are available?

We offer research opportunities in areas like Digital Transformation, Healthcare IS, E-Government Solutions, and Sustainable IT. Contact faculty members for specific projects.
